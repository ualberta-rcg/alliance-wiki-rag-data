Research data management is a crucial aspect of the data lifecycle and ensures that data is discoverable, accessible and reusable during and following the completion of a project. In Canada, [https://alliancecan.ca/ the Alliance] is tasked with:

<!--T:3-->
*Developing a national research data culture
*Fostering a community of practice for research data
*Building national research data services and infrastructure

<!--T:4-->
...to better support academic researchers and institutions in building capacities and managing their data assets. To learn more or receive support with your research data management [https://alliancecan.ca/en/contact-us contact the Alliance directly], or speak to your local regional partner and they will get you connected.
